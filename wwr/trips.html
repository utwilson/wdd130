Featured Trips

Canyon Scenic Float

Beginner Half-day • Class II–III May–September

Perfect for families and first-timers. Enjoy gentle splashes, wildlife viewing, and towering sandstone walls while our guides handle the rowing.

  • Duration: ~4 hours (10:00–14:00)
  • Includes snacks, PFD, and river shoes
  • Great for ages 6+ (50 lbs minimum)

Pine Gorge Express

Intermediate Full-day • Class III–IV Season: April–October

A favorite among returning guests, this trip features fun wave trains, surf spots, and a full lunch along the river.

  • Duration: 7–8 hours
  • Lunch + drinks included
  • Recommended ages 12+ with moderate paddling ability
